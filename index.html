Terms of Use

Lightchain Archives is a decentralized interface to the Lightchain blockchain. The developer does not host, store, or control any content uploaded through this app.

The following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content involving the exploitation of minors
  • Content that is illegal under applicable law
  • Copyrighted material you do not have rights to share
  • Content intended to harass, threaten, or harm others

All uploads are permanent. Your wallet address is permanently linked to everything you store. There is no way to delete content from the blockchain.

🔒 About Private memories: Marking a memory Private hides it from the public archive and browse view in this app. Only your connected wallet will see it here. However, all data is permanently stored on the Lightchain blockchain — a public distributed ledger. Anyone who queries the blockchain directly can access the raw data. Private in this app means hidden from Lightchain Archives, not erased from the chain. Do not upload anything you would never want on a public ledger.
