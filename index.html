Your Time Capsule is Forever

Even if this website disappears someday — your time capsule lives on the blockchain, safe forever.

Your photos, videos, and stories are stored directly on the Lightchain blockchain — a permanent public record that nobody can erase, not even us. This app is just a friendly window to view them. If the window breaks, the memories behind it are still there.
