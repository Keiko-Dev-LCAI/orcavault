How It Works
No cloud. No IPFS. Everything lives on Lightchain — forever.
1
Connect Your Wallet
Use Trust Wallet or any EVM wallet. Your wallet is your account — no signup, no email, no password.
2
Create Your Archive
Pick a template, give it a name, and deploy it to the Lightchain blockchain in one transaction.
3
Add Memories
Upload photos, videos, audio, or text. Files are compressed and stored as blockchain calldata — gas costs pennies.
4
Share Anywhere
Share your archive link via WhatsApp, social media, or QR code. Anyone can view it — no wallet needed.

Terms of Use

Audio & Visual Archives is a decentralized interface to the Lightchain blockchain. The developer does not host, store, or control any content uploaded through this app.

The following content is strictly prohibited:
